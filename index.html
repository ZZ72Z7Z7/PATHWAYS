About This Initiative

Pathways for Arts Educator Development was created in response to Proposition 28 (The Arts and Music in Schools Act), to address the shortage of qualified arts educators in California's public schools. This initiative brings together universities, professional associations, community-based organizations and advocacy groups to illuminate clear, accessible pathways into arts education careers.

Whether you want to become a credentialed classroom teacher or work as a teaching artist in schools and community settings, this tool will help you find programs, resources and professional development opportunities that match your background and support your goals.
