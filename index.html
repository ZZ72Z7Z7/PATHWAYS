About This Initiative

Pathways for Arts Educator Development was created to address the growing shortage of qualified arts educators in California's public schools. This initiative brings together universities, county arts commissions, teaching artist organizations, and advocacy groups to provide clear, accessible pathways into arts education careers.

Whether you want to become a credentialed classroom teacher or work as a teaching artist in schools and community settings, this tool helps you find the programs, funding, and professional networks that match your background and goals.
